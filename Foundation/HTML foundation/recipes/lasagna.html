lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish of pasta sheets layered with fillings and baked. The word "lasagna" can refer to the pasta sheets or the dish itself

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
